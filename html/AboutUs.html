About Us

Discover our story, weekly training achievements, and hear from our dancers!

Our Club Origin

It started with a spark—students who believed dance could be more than movement. They came together, pitched their vision, and brought the NYP Dance Club to life. Since its launch, the club has pulsed through campus with energy, creativity, and connection, becoming a vibrant space where talent thrives and stories unfold through motion.

Hear from our dancers

Gupta K.

From cohort of DBFT 2023

"Training sessions were tough sometimes, but we always had fun. Everyone looked out for each other, and you could really feel the team spirit, especially before performances. It wasn't just about dance, it was about growing together."

Alicia Walters

From cohort of DAAA 2024

"Before joining NYP Dance Club, I never thought I would enjoy performing on stage. But the training, the people, and the energy really changed that. Every session was a chance to improve and just vibe with the crew. Looking back, it's not just the dance I remember. It is the late-night rehearsals, the inside jokes, and the feeling of being part of something bigger."

Marcus Tan Jian Wei

From cohort of DIT 2020

"Joining NYP Dance Club was one of the best decisions I made in polytechnic. I came in with barely any dance experience but the seniors were super supportive. Every training pushed me to grow, not just as a dancer, but as a person. The performances and battles made the bond we built feel like family. This CCA really gave me confidence and lifelong memories."
