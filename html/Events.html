Past Highlights

Break the Beat '24

A signature event by the Dance Club, spotlighting freestyle expression, choreographic battles, and collaborative performances. It brings together dancers from across the school to celebrate movement, music, and creative competition.

Inter-school Dance Competition

A thrilling showcase of rhythm, style, and teamwork, where our dancers lit up the stage with precision and passion. Beyond the moves, it was a journey of growth, building confidence, creativity, and a powerful sense of unity during rehearsals.

Singapore Youth Festival

SYF was a chance for our dancers to grow, bond, and shine. From early rehearsals to the final bow, they poured heart and discipline into every formation—bringing our story to life on stage.

Senior Dance Off 2024

It was a night dedicated to our graduating dancers. Our seniors took their final chance to own the floor, stepping into crew battles that showcased not just their skills, but their growth, grit, and passion. Every move was a reflection of how far they have come.
